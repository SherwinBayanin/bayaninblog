Christmas in Baguio

by Sherwin Bayanin

Posted on August 24, 2013 at 9:00 PM

Previous Next

It’s that time of year at the Baguio Country Club when it treats its visitors to an unforgettable “Christmas Village” scene, an annual tradition that has made it a sentimental favorite spot for Yuletide revelers.

Christmas village impressed once more with falling snowflakes and model of houses and colorful christmas trees which amazed visitors from other provinces as well as the residents of Baguio City.

With different theme every year like last years theme, "How to train your dragon" which made the visitors amazed.The landscape also features famous Disney characters and princesses.

Visitors and children also enjoy the artificial snow (which are bubbles). Visitors also enjoy the presence of friendly staff. Stores and variety of stores can also be found in the area.

The Entrance fee for Adults is 100 Pesos and 40 Pesos for childrens ages from 4-12 years old.
